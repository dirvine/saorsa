About Saorsa Labs

Located in the historic stables of Barr, on the edge of the Galloway Forest in Rural South Ayrshire, Saorsa Labs is at the forefront of AI and robotics research. Our focus is on developing innovative solutions that enhance human lives through the integration of caring robots and advanced AI systems.

Research Areas

Carer Bots

Developing assistive robots focused on supporting human care needs, emphasizing practical functionality over complex humanoid designs.

Neuromorphic Computing

Exploring brain-inspired computing architectures to create more efficient and adaptive AI systems.

Analogue Computing

Investigating novel approaches to computation using analogue systems for enhanced efficiency and performance.

Our Facilities

Buic Workshop

Our metalwork facility, named after Scottish inventor James Buic, specializing in precision robotics fabrication.

Dunlop Laboratory

Advanced polymers and plastics research facility, honoring John Boyd Dunlop's innovative spirit.

Maxwell Electronics

State-of-the-art electronics laboratory, named after James Clerk Maxwell, pioneering electromagnetic theory.
